History

Islamabad was selected as a suitable site for the new capital of Pakistan in 1958 and the move was complete in 1966. The city was carefully planned from the very beginning by the then-president -- Ayub Khan. Rawalpindi is a twin city connecting it from one side and the other sides are surrounded by the Margalla Hills.

About Us
